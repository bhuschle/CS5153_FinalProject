About Us


Tech N' Stuff was started by Shabnam Mustafa, Ethan Chappel, Brandon Huschle, Jesus Perez in January 2022. At Tech N' Stuff, our purpose is to enrich lives through technology.


We're building brighter futures through technology.

Every teen has dreams, and we're working to make sure they have the opportunity to achieve them. Our programs connect young people with resources to realize their potential and channel their talent, energy and ideas into brighter futures.


Environmental Sustainability

From carbon reduction to e-waste recycling, we're working with our customers to create a more sustainable future. We've pledged to be carbon neutral by 2040


Inclusion & Diversity

We're committed to creating a more inclusive future, both inside our company and in our communities. Tech N' Stuff's purpose is to enrich lives through technology, and we know we can't do that without an inclusive culture and diverse employees who reflect our customers.
